Landmarks

1975 KSCST the brain child of visionaries in Indian Institute of Science and administrators of the Government of Karnataka was established as registered society.
1977 KSCST launched Student Projects Programme (SPP), the first of this kind in India.
1981 KSCST jointly with Department of Science and Technology, Government of India, organised first All India Meet of State Councils.
1982 Community biogas plant set up at Pura village, Kunigal district (113 Kms. from Bangalore) with a view to provide cooking gas for the villages.
1984 240 m 2 solar pond for low grade heat extraction established at the Indian Institute of Science, Bangalore. Astra Ole an, improved fuel efficient wood burning stove, developed in an earlier project at KSCST is adopted by the Government of Karnataka for large scale dissemination all over the state.
1985 Sisal industries demonstration unit established at Ungra to process one TPD sisal leaves to manufacture rope, paper and Hecogenin.
1986 Cell for Renewable Energy Dissemination Activities (CREDA) setup at KSCST to co-ordinate dissemination of renewable energy technologies.
1988 KSCST took initiative to setup the Drought Monitoring Cell.
1989 First 100 kW Wood Gasifier based power generation system commissioned at Andaman & Nicobar islands. Development of Powdery Biomass Gasifier project was launched.
1990 Training programmes on Alternative Building Technologies launched.
1991 Launched "SNAPSHOT" quarterly newsletter for an information exchange among state councils in the country.
1992 Natural Resources Data Management System (NRDMS) Programme was launched in Karnataka.
1993 District Committees for Science & Technology (DCST) established earlier made operational on a regular basis. Testing of 100 kW gasifier by Swiss authorities for their use in Europe marks one more first in technology transfer from India.
1994 Installed the 40 kW microhydel unit at Keregodu village near Mandya.
1995 Preparation of Zoning Atlas for Siting of Industries was initiated in Mysore district. The project supported by Central Pollution Control Board, Government of India.
1996 Ministry of Non-conventional Energy Sources [MNES] sponsored project for successfully removing H 2 S from industrial and municipal waste effluent was initiated.
1997 Projects on Draught Animal Power and Bio-diversity was initiated.
1998 Installed Microhydel unit at Elneer. Established a secretariat to coordinate the activities of Indo-Norwegian Environmental Programme (INEP) activities. Project is coordinated by a Senior scale KAS officer from Government of Karnataka.
1999 Launched a project to study and establish R & D units in Small Scale Industries. Implementation of INEP was on.
2000 Projects on Organic Sludge Utility in Wasteland Development
2001 Projects on Agro biodiversity. Involvement in developing biodiversity strategy and action plan.
2002 Initiated Scientists - Students Interaction Programme (SSIP). Launching of Biomass Energy for Rural India (BERI) Programme.
2003 Launching of Rainwater Harvesting programme.
2004 Project on Identification of Strategies for mapping of S&T needs in the State.
2005 Starting of Patent Information Centre (PIC) with support of TIFAC, DST, Government of India.
Launching of RDPR-KSCST Rainwater Harvesting Programme for rural Karnataka.
National Meet of State S&T Ministers, Heads of S&T Councils and S&T Departments.
2006 Establishment of Rainwater Harvesting Cell at KSCST
Initiation of providing technical consultancy services by KSCST on commercial terms to institutions.
2007 Established Karnataka State Spatial Data Infrastructure (KSSDI), with support of Government of Karnataka and Government of India.
2008 Initiated projects on - Resource Mapping, Metal Recovery from E-waste and Utilisation of Iron Ore Tailings in Concrete.
2009 Launched Karnataka Geoportal - Inaugurated by Union Minister for Science and Technology.
2010 Initiated and organized the first Science and Technology Conference.
Organised Trainers Training Programme for NAM Countries.
Indo-EU Workshop on Water Resources
2011 Establishment of Biofuel Cell to co-ordinate R&D activity.
Evaluation and Monitoring watershed activities under IWMP Programme.
