Strengths of KSCST

  • Presence of a dedicated group of scientific and technical personnel in all the core areas of activity.
  • Excellent interaction with eminent Scientific and Technical Personnel of the Indian Institute of Science due to its location within the campus.
  • Strong linkages with the S&T personnel in other premier R&D institutions in Karnataka.
  • Closer interaction with Government of Karnataka.
  • Ability to attract funds from multilateral agencies.
  • Freedom to partner with multilateral agencies - viz. NGOs, Industry, Academia and Government.

Institutions spawned by KSCST.

Various S&T programmes, seeded and nurtured at KSCST, have led to spawning of independent S & T institutions in the State. The following are the important ones:

  • Karnataka Rajya Vijnana Parishat (KRVP)
  • Karnataka State Natural Disaster Monitoring Centre (KSNDMC)
  • Karnataka Renewable Energy Development Limited (KREDL)
